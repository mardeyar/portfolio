    StatSense

    Flutter

    Android

    iOS

    Windows

    An application designed to assist NHL fantasy league managers with picking up and dropping players. StatSense pulls the NHL schedule directly from the public API and gives quick, at a glance information as to how many games each team is playing that week and how many of those games fall on days with less than half the total NHL teams playing: 'off days'.

    Gravity

    Java

    Mobile

    AOSP

    Sensors

    Another mobile development project, this one uses the gravity sensors in an Android device to move balls around on a screen by tilting the device. The aim of the game is to tilt the phone towards the moving line along the top of the screen and make the balls hit the line. Each collision makes the ball disappear and increments the scoreCount. Tap the screen to add more balls.
